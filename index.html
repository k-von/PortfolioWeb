About Me

Amazing journeys start with a single step...

I am a Southern California resident, and I have lived here for most of my life. I have always had passion for computers and creativity, and had aspirations to do something great in the world of bytes and bits. I often joke that when I was born, I came into this world with a keyboard. There's just something amazing about how the interface between a human and machine can create so many amazing ideas. This is where I have often spent time pondering about the different ways I can make life better and easier for people by streamlining or automating certain task. In this age of technology you can see that we rely heavility on computers and what they can do for us. I love finding out what makes a website tick, or getting tickled by the amazing aethetics of a website. There's just something comforting and enticing about a well designed website, that perfectly pairs functionality with aesthetics. I will use my talents to make your site speak to your audience and give them a reason to always come back.
