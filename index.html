About Me

Amazing journeys start with a single step...

My name is Kayvon, and I have been a Southern California resident living in the Los Angeles area for the majority of my life. I enjoy a very active business and personal life and am always looking to stretch myself to live up to my full potential while on this planet. Professionally, I have a very eclectic and interesting work history, holding positions such as a data processor to a technical analyst, software engineer, programmer, financial analyst, realtor, and now, a full-stack website developer. I have been fortunate to enjoy a rewarding professional life. Despite my diverse background, the common thread has always been fostering a passion and creative acumen for technology, in particular for computers. I often joke that I was born into this world with a keyboard. I have found it amazing how computers have, within a relatively short amount of time, become essentially an extension of ourselves exposed to the world. How did we even anticipate that social and computers would go hand in hand?

Although being a computer geek has seemingly been a persona I always have aspired to; I am a real estate broker by trade. I have been in the industry for over 11 years, and have used my technical savviness to keep myself ahead of the crowd and create a profitable business that leverages technology to gain market share.

Having been involved in real estate for good part of my professional life has made me understand consumer behavior and crowd psychology. Also, real estate has gifted me with the skills necessary to understand consumer habits and information requirements when using the internet as a resource.

Understanding how the business rules interface with the online audience is one of my fortes and hallmark talents that I bring forth to the design.

Teaming up with me guarantees, you will have a functional and aesthetically enticing website that will deliver to the end consumer your value proposition, which will, in turn, leading to repeat customers/clients, and more business.

Go ahead and check out my website and see some of my ongoing projects. If you feel we can be a good fit working together, go ahead and contact me to learn how I can help you reach your objective in the most efficient and expeditious way.
